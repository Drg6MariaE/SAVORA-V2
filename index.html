About Us

Since its inception in 2015, Savora has been synonymous with culinary excellence and timeless elegance. Nestled in the heart of the city, Savora is a sanctuary for those who crave unforgettable dining experiences.

Helmed by our visionary Executive Chef, Luca Moretti, Savora blends traditional techniques with innovative flavors. With over 15 years of international culinary expertise, Chef Moretti has curated a menu that delights even the most discerning palates.

Known for our signature truffle risotto, herb-crusted rack of lamb, and an exquisite selection of desserts, Savora redefines the art of fine dining.

About Our Chef – Luca Moretti

Since the inception of Savora, one name has been at the heart of every dish served: Chef Luca Moretti. With over 15 years of international culinary experience, Chef Luca brings a blend of precision, creativity, and soul to every plate.

His journey began in a small family kitchen in Florence, where he first fell in love with the art of flavor. After training in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, he developed a signature style that fuses classic European techniques with bold, modern innovation.

“Each ingredient has a voice. My role is to help it sing.”

Under his leadership, Savora has become a destination for those who crave refined, meaningful food. From the delicate balance of our truffle risotto to the bold elegance of our herb-crusted rack of lamb, every dish reflects his commitment to authenticity, artistry, and hospitality.

More than a chef, Luca is a curator of moments — carefully crafting not just meals, but memories.

Accessibility Features: Dining for Everyone

At Savora, inclusivity is at the heart of our dining experience. We are dedicated to ensuring every guest enjoys a comfortable visit. Our features include:

Step-Free Access & Ramps

Smooth, ramp-equipped access for easy entry and exit for all mobility devices. Wheelchair-accessible tables and high-chair options for children.

Reserved Parking & Easy Drop-Off Zones

Convenient accessible parking and drop-off zones close to the entrance for those with mobility challenges.

Service Animal Accommodations

Your service animals are always welcome, with designated seating areas to ensure comfort for both you and your companion.
